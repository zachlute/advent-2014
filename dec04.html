A Light in the Darkness

The Rev. Marilynn Brown

Isaiah 9:1-7

This painting was done for the season of Advent for Faith Lutheran Church, Bellingham WA, to hang in the sanctuary as a reminder, that during this darkest time of the year, and during the dark times in our lives, The Prince of Peace is always available to be reborn in our hearts, offering us hope, peace and glimpses of eternal light.
